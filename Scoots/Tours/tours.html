Explore Cozumel!

Isla Cozumel

The name Cozumel was derived from the Mayan "Cuzamil" or "Ah Cuzamil Peten" in full, which means "the island of swallows" (Spanish: Isla de las Golondrinas).

Diving

Take advantage of Cozumel’s clear waters and sea life diversity! There are many services throughout the island at different price rates. Whether you are a starter or an experienced diver there is a place for you!

Beaches

From the most popular beaches to beaches that you want to explore, check our list for the best beaches in Cozumel! Come explore the beauties of the island while having a great time, making the most out of your time here!

Activities

Enjoy the different activities all over the island. Water sports, golf, spa, restaurants and much more, choose how you want to spend your time while staying at the island. There are activities for the family, for a group of friends or if you want to spend some time with yourself, there’s something for everyone!

Native History

Come and check Mayan ruins and ancient native history sites. These are the destination for many tourist and a great opportunity to learn a little more about the history of this wonderful place!
