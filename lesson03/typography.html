Typography

The typography used in the Smart Weather website is Mariweather Sans and Mariweather. Merriweather Sans is a low-contrast semi-condensed sans-serif text typeface family and Merriweather was designed to be a text face that is pleasant to read on screens. It features a very large x height, slightly condensed letterforms, a mild diagonal stress, sturdy serifs and open forms.
